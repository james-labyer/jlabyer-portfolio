Octavia E. Butler

Trail-blazing science fiction author

Octavia Estelle Butler was an American science fiction author. She recieved Hugo and Nebula awards and a MacArthur fellowship during her career. Her work used science fiction to critique modern social issues such as racism and sexism. Today she is considered a highly influential author and many modern science fiction authors list her work as a formative influence on their own fiction.

Butler's major works include several influential science fiction series:

  • Patternist series (published 1976 - 1984)
  • Xenogenesis, or Lilith's Brood, series (published 1987 - 1989)
  • Parable, or Earthseed series (published 1993 - 1998)
